# Page Strategy: ridge-wallet-preview
Brand: Ridge
Page type: physical product landing page
Built: 2026-03-30
Confidence: medium

## 1. Core decision
- Product / service: Ridge Wallet 2.0 minimalist wallet
- Offer: Slim RFID-blocking wallet with lifetime warranty and 99-day risk reversal
- Audience: Men carrying bulky bifolds who are willing to pay for durability and design
- Awareness stage: Solution-aware
- CTA action: Buy now
- CTA destination: Product detail page / checkout path
- Conversion goal: Direct purchase

## 2. Mechanism map
- Primary buyer job: Carry essential cards and cash in a slimmer, more durable form factor
- Primary pain: Traditional wallets are bulky, wear out, and feel outdated
- Failed alternatives: Cheap slim wallets that break, jam cards, or feel flimsy
- Unique mechanism: Rigid metal frame + dual-side card access + integrated RFID shielding
- Why the mechanism works: Frame architecture reduces bulk while preserving retention and access
- What must be shown visually: Wallet silhouette, card access, premium material finish, pocket-friendly scale
- What must be explained verbally: Capacity, access system, durability, warranty, return policy

## 3. Claims control
### Allowed claims
- Holds 1-12 cards in 6mm — derived_source
- RFID-blocking built into the frame — derived_source
- Lifetime warranty — verified_source
- 99-day trial / return framing — derived_source

### Forbidden claims
- Any unsupported medical or posture benefit
- Any invented customer count, revenue, or celebrity usage

### Claims requiring proof near them
- Reviews volume
- Wallets sold
- Years in business
