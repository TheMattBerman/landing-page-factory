## 1. Core decision
- Product / service: AI ad intelligence + creative generation tool
- Offer: Analyze competitor ads psychologically → generate ready-to-run brand creatives. From Growth ($149/mo) to Managed ($3,000/mo).
- Audience: Heads of Growth, Creative Strategists, Founders — primarily DTC/B2C brands running paid social
- Awareness stage: Problem-aware to solution-aware (they know competitor ads are outperforming; don't know this tool)
- CTA action: Get Early Access / See It In Action
- CTA destination: Sign up / demo flow (exact URL not confirmed — point to stealads.ai/signup or app.stealads.ai)
- Conversion goal: Email capture / account creation for early access

## 2. Mechanism map
- Primary buyer job: Beat competitors on paid social without burning weeks on manual research and creative iteration
- Primary pain: Hours lost to inspiration scrolling, copying surface-level aesthetics without understanding WHY ads work
- Failed alternatives: Manual swipe files, ad libraries, hiring expensive creative teams who still guess
- Unique mechanism: AI psychological deconstruction — breaks down the visual AND psychological elements driving ad performance, then generates creatives engineered to the same psychological triggers
- Why the mechanism works: Psychology drives why people stop, click, and buy — not surface aesthetics. Competitors copying aesthetics miss the real lever.
- What must be shown visually: Product UI — ad analysis dashboard with psychological breakdown panels, side-by-side competitor ad vs. generated creative
- What must be explained verbally: The "decode → generate" loop and why it beats swipe-file copying

## 3. Claims control
### Allowed claims
- "Your competitors' ads are winning" — observed, provenance from brief
- "Decodes the psychology behind high-performing ads" — mechanism, observed
- "Generates ready-to-run creative for your brand" — benefit, observed
- Testimonials: exact wording only, attributed as shown

### Forbidden claims
- Any specific conversion lift percentages (not in source)
- "X% faster" or time-saved stats (not in source)
- Named brand clients (not verified)
- Any guarantee language not in source

### Claims requiring proof near them
- "Better than anything I've seen" — Chris L. quote must be attributed to GM @ Fitness Brand
- All testimonials need name + title adjacent

## 5. Preservation plan
### Trust cues to preserve
- Named testimonials with exact wording and titles
- Early Access framing (urgency + exclusivity)
- Pricing tiers (signals real, live product)
- "Steal" verb in brand identity — this is the brand's personality, preserve it

### Visual conventions to preserve
- Dark/off-black as dominant: signals SaaS, tech, power
- Orange (#FF5733) accent: aggressive, urgent, different from typical SaaS blue
- High contrast between dark UI and page background

### Language to preserve
- "steal the psychology" — exact mechanism phrase
- "ready-to-run creative" — specificity of output
- "your competitors' ads are winning" — confrontational opener
- "decode" / "decodes" — mechanism language

### Deviations allowed
- Replace Montserrat/Open Sans with Satoshi + DM Sans (anti-slop type improvement)
- Use off-black #0a0a0a instead of pure black #000000
- Warm bone background instead of pure white/grey
- Asymmetric layout instead of centered-everything
